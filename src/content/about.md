# About
In the Spring of 2006, I found a diary containing the only surviving documentation of a religious doomsday cult that settled in the Washington area at least a hundred years ago by my approximation. There is no information available about this “church” online or through any resource or database in Washington state, this journal is the only evidence of its existence.

At first, I also didn’t believe that the diary was real, that it was nothing but some elaborate draft for a book or something like that. It wasn’t until I noticed the first clues woven into the entries, referencing places I know, places I remember seeing before, that I started to take the journal seriously.

 This diary is filled to the brim with mysteries: the identity of the diary’s author, the remains of the cult or their church, the nature of the angel they call "Lucet", or what happened to them after their prophecy are all unknown. This book is my only lead to answering these questions and uncovering the truth for myself.

It’s not enough for me to just transcribe these entries. If I want to find answers, I have to find this cult, or whatever may be left of it, and I’ll have to use the clues in the diary to do it. This website is my best attempt to document my investigation and where it takes me; I intend to follow this rabbit hole to its conclusion.

I’ll need your help, too. If you have any information related to this case, I’ve created a contact form to reach me. Any and all leads are appreciated, I hope to see the internet come together to help me solve this mystery.


Hope to hear from you soon,
A friend.


P.S. The music you’re hearing is not random background music. This diary doesn’t just contain the only written record of the “Lucet” cult, but the only written music as well: musical sketches left behind by the author in a second section of the diary entitled “For Those Who Are Free”. What you’re listening to are my interpretations of these sketches based on the notes in the journal, some more faithful than others. 

I hope I did you justice, whoever you are.
